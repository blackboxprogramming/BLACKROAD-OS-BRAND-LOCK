Frequently asked questions

Can I self-host everything? −
Yes. BlackRoad OS is fully self-hostable. Every component runs on your own hardware with no cloud dependency required.
What hardware do I need? +
Any ARM64 or x86 device works. We recommend Raspberry Pi 5 with Hailo-8 accelerators for optimal AI performance.
Is there a free trial? +
Pro plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime? +
Yes. Changes take effect immediately and billing is prorated.
